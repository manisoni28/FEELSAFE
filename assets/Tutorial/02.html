Prime Contacts

As said before, FeelSafe sends alert message and location to contacts.
You can set your app-contacts in the contacts page.The Contact page is shown on clicking the Group-button on the top right in home screen.

The app must have atleast two contacts for proper functioning. These Contacts are called PRIME CONTACTS


**It is recommended that important persons and people who respond quickly are set as Prime Contacts.

The Prime Contacts are listed first in the Contacts page with a crown symbol , then the rest contacts follows. You can set as many contacts as you want. You can call or message the contact-person directly from here.

The pencil like button on the top enables you to remove/edit the contact information like name,number and photo. you can also change/switch the Prime contacts in editing page.
