Location Features

In FeelSafe you can see your own Location or send it to some one through SMS or Whatsapp.
Below is the screenshot of Location tab.

If the reciever also has FeelSafe, he/she gets direct Location Alerts from the app, showing the map view.



To send your location first select a medium SMS or whatsapp and click SEND MY LOCATION.

For sending through SMS you should also SELECT CONTACT which You can enter manually or select one from the app contacts.

SHOW MY LOCATION button shows your location in map view.
